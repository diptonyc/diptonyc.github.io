Ice climbing & hiking on glaciers

Glacier Experience is a small group tour that offers an easy and accessible glacier hike on the stunning Sólheimajökull glacier in Iceland. Led by an experienced glacier guide, this adventure is perfect for those looking to explore the icy wilderness safely and up close—no previous experience needed. The tour is available year-round, allowing you to witness the glacier’s ever-changing beauty in summer’s soft light or winter’s frosty glow.

Glacier Experience is a small group experience... Add even more adventure to your stay in Iceland and come explore this amazing crawling glacier with us.

Sólheimajökull glacier is an outlet from the mighty Mýrdalsjökull... during summertime, a glacier lagoon forms in front of the glacier only adding to the charm of the location.
